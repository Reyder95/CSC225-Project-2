Why an Art Shop?

I personally find art to be really fascinating, and the fact that I can bring people to showcase their talents and personalities all while making a living is really special to me. On this site you'll be able to find lots of talented artists and see what they can do.

What type of art do you showcase?

Rather than more realistic art, I like to showcase and sell the more phantasmical art. I like the fantasy element of art, and in my opinion drawing what isn't real is part of the fun of a fantasy world. In where we currently live, we don't have such beautiful landscapes easily accessible, so art like this can help fill that void!

What is the price range?

We sell art from all around, so the price range is very wide. You can either buy simple pieces for as little as $15 - $30, or some incredible art piece for a few hundred! It's really up to what you, the consumer, wants to spend. We will definitely have a piece that fits your budget!
